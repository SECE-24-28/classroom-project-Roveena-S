Chemistry

Chemistry is the branch of science that deals with elements, compounds, mixtures, and how they react. It explains everything from the air we breathe to the food we eat. It includes concepts like atomic structure, bonding, reactions, and chemical equations.

Mathematics

Mathematics helps us understand quantity, patterns, and relationships. Topics include algebra, geometry, calculus, trigonometry, and statistics. It builds logical thinking and problem-solving skills essential for science and engineering.
